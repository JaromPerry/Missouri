Kansas City

Welcome to Kansas City, Missouri! With over 500,000 residents, this urban hub was incorporated in 1850 and lies in the western region of the state. It blends urban and suburban lifestyles, boasting a diverse economy and slightly higher-than-average incomes. Discover its vibrant culture, delicious cuisine, and rich history in the heart of the Midwest.
