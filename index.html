The Show me State

Discover Missouri, the "Show Me State," where vibrant cities like St. Louis and Kansas City pulse with culture and flavor. Dive into the heart of the Ozarks for outdoor adventures amidst stunning landscapes. Explore Civil War battlegrounds, historic towns, and the hometown of Mark Twain. Missouri invites you to uncover its rich history, natural beauty, and warm hospitality.

Fun Facts

  • The state of Missouri was named after a tribe of Sioux Indians of the state called the Missouris. The word "Missouri" means "wooden canoe people" or "he of the big canoe."
  • The Gateway Arch in St. Louis is the tallest man-made national monument in the United States. It stands 630 feet tall.
